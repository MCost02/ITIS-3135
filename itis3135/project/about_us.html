About Us

“Integrity” is not just a name. It is the foundation of our character.

Tia is a certified court reporter, owner, and managing member of Integrity Court Reporting, LLC, a multi-service court reporting firm based in Kennesaw, Georgia. Tia attended Brown College of Court Reporting in Atlanta, Georgia, where she graduated with honors and obtained her associate’s degree in court reporting. Prior to becoming a nationally certified court reporter, Tia obtained her bachelor’s degree in business administration from Johnson and Wales University in Miami, Florida, and has been in the legal field of investigation services for over ten years.
