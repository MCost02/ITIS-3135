Professional, Reliable, and Honest

Integrity Court Reporting is a full-service attorney support agency providing services throughout North Georgia. We offer services that range from court reporting to litigation support services and more. Our team of reporters are committed to high-quality services. We have the best court reporters, videographers, and translators. Our team is committed to ensuring our client's deadlines, accommodating their needs, requests, and expectations are exceeded.

Court Reporting Our team of court reporters are certified on a state and/or national level and experienced in various types of proceedings. As a court reporter, we are professionally trained to take down verbatim proceedings creating an accurate record of depositions, hearings, meetings, conventions, arbitrations, interviews, conferences, and produce an accurate transcript in a timely manner. Our reporters punctual, versatile to their work environment, and attentive to all the details of the proceeding.
Legal Videography We provide a professional and accurate video recording of proceedings. Our video recordings are a great way to supplement any written transcripts. We'll help document your meeting and legal proceeding through our high-tech video and sound equipment. Our videographers will give you more than a fragment of information; they'll give you facial expressions, pauses and documents what was said in your meetings
Telepehonic Conducting a telephonic deposition can be a good way to cut costs and save time travel. Often, deposing witnesses can be located a long-distance away, out of state, or a witness is unable to travel because of physical limitations or illness. This is an issue that many attorneys face. Integrity Court Reporting can save you time and money when parties aren't able to travel. We also guarantee state-of-the-art phone equipment to ensure the best audio possible. Our audio is clear, free of skips and random distortion.
Interpreter Integrity Court Reporting, LLC, provides interpretation services in all languages. Our Interpreters are certified and experienced in their respective language(s) and can provide simultaneous verbatim interpretation during legal proceedings. Upon request, we can arrange for an Interpreter to accompany our Court Reporter to ensure clear and effective communication between speakers, resulting in a clean and accurate transcript.
Video/Remote Service Throughout the years, video and audio services have become more reliable and dependent upon for litigations, such as depositions, trials, hearings, arbitrations, and mediations. Often, traveling for legal proceedings may not be ideal. Scheduling conflicts, travel costs, long flight times, or weather delays are some issues that can be encountered. Integrity Court Reporting can efficiently connect you and other participants through our secure and high-speed video conferencing services. We also provide mobile and video/text streaming capabilities.
Expedited Transcripts Integrity Court Reporting offers fast, professional, and cost-effective transcript services. We offer both standard and expedited services. Our trained, certified court reporters and transcribers can deliver completed transcripts as soon as you need them, including same-day completion.
Conference Room We offer several deposition suites that are conveniently located around the Metro Atlanta area. All of our conference rooms are fully equipped with the latest technology for audio and video communication, large video monitors, high-speed internet, Wi-Fi, comfortable and spacious seating that can be tailored to accommodate any group size. If there are any special requests, please let us know. We will try our best to make it happen.
