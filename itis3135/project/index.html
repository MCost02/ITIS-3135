We Service Law Firms, Corporations, Government Agencies, & Insurance Companies

Integrity Court Reporting, LLC, is a full-service court reporting agency based in the Atlanta area. We provide statewide court reporting, videography, video conferencing, legal interpretation, and transcription services.

At Integrity Court Reporting, we take pride in the production of our work and are dedicated to providing you with accurate and timely transcripts with the professional and personalized service that meets and exceeds your expectations. Whether you need coverage for a deposition, hearing, examination under oath, board meeting, or interviews, we are here to accommodate your reporting needs.

“Integrity” is not just a name. It is the foundation of our character: professional, reliable, and honest. We adhere to the Code of Ethics and commit to handling each case with the utmost confidentiality, and we serve as an impartial party to every case.

What can we do for you?

Court Reporting

Our court reporters are certified, realtime capable, and experienced in all aspects verbatim reporting.

Videography

We offer high definition video and recording. Our videos will help capture body language, facial expression, intonation, gestures, etc.

Telepehonic

We offer simple and secure ways to communicate and interview witnesses.

Video/Remote Service

Our conference rooms are equipped with HD video screens, high-speed wifi, and high-quality speakers for a successful meeting.

interpretation

Our translators offer a full range of language interpretation to provide you with clear communication between speakers of different languages.

Online Repository

Instant document retrieval. We understand how important and time pressing your transcripts are. We offer several delivery options in a safe, secure, and confidential way.
