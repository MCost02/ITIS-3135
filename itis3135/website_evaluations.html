Website Evaluations

Poorly Designed Site: Ling's Cars

This seems to be a website for an auto dealer. The website is very cluttered and hard to navigate.

  • Contrast: The contrast on this site is awful. The floral background, the bright extremely bright colors, and the animations make it painful on the eyes and frankly, very ugly.
  • Repitition: As far as repetition, the only thing that seemed consistent is the vehicle manufacturers on the left side of the page with specific listings in the middle. However, it is still very hard to follow and the rest of the page doesn't seem to follow any sort of pattern.
  • Alignment: Although the alignment on this site isn't the most appealing, it is consistent throughout the various sections. However, since all the vehicle makes and models are left-aligned, there is a lot of blank space in the middle and right side as you scroll further down the page.
  • Proximity: Everything is in close proximity to each other and in sections, but it is still an eyesore. While the middle section with specific vehicle listings is grouped nicely in a grid, the vehicle manufacturer column on the left needs some work. This would be better suited with a scrollable section or collapsible menu rather than scrolling down the entire page.

Well Designed Site: Genesis Block

This appears to be a website for blockchain technology. It has a simple, yet elegant design that is very appealing to the eye.

  • Contrast: The contrast on this site is quite nice, consisting of white text on a black background, with occasional accents of blue, green, and purple. Everything is easy to read and it is easy on the eyes.
  • Repitition: The repetition on this site is great, with consistent use of the same font, sizes, and colors throughout the entire website.
  • Alignment: The site uses left-aligned text, with visual elements and spacing in between. This creates an easy to read, visually appealing website.
  • Proximity: Related content is nicely grouped together with great use of space between different sections. The scrolling animation is an excellent touch as you transition through different blocks of text.
